PRIVACY POLICY

Thank you for visiting www.pcbgogo.com. PCBGOGO is committed to protect your online privacy. We will not collect your personal information when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that information for us.
PCBGOGO's Commitment:

A Protecting all PCB and PCB Assembly data provided to us by customers from being stolen, compromised, or used in violation with this policy.

B. PCBGOGO will not share, release, publish, disclose, rent, or sell any personally identifiable information to any third parties.

C. Providing transparency for our users what PCB data we collect and what we do with including what access third parties have to user data

D.for what purpose. Informing users if their data is compromised, either by failure to protect PCB and PCBA data or by order to surrender data to a legal authority, to the fullest extent we are lawfully able.

Information Collected
If you browse our website or upload information, we will automatically store the following information about your visit:

A The web page(s) you visit

B. The date and time you accessed our website

C. The type of operating system used to access our website

D.for what purpose. The type of browser used to access our website

E. The Internet domain and IP address to which you accessed our website

F. The search term(s) used in your search engine

We collect this information to learn about the number of visitors to PCBGOGO website. This helps us make our website friendly for more users. We do not record information about individuals and their visits, and will not identify you personally.
Emailing to Users
Emails will be sent to PCB and PCB assembly customers when orders are placed, shipped, or ready for local pickup-ed. We may also be contact them when an order requires payment before shipping.
Customers will be notified if we believe their privacy has been compromised, if it is within our power to do so. For example, if we discover a customer’s account user login information has been exposed elsewhere on the web, or if we are compelled to disclose information to law enforcement.
We may occasionally send you an email that you do not request or trigger with an action of any kind in order to promote a certain aspect of our site, products, or services.
Interaction between PCBGOGO and User
If you email us, chat with us, or call us on the telephone for support, we can and most of the time will record the interaction. This is done so for training and audit purposes and the private conversations between customers and support staff are never sold to or shared with third parties (except when a third party tool, such as email or our chat service, which is used to make the interaction possible).
About Website Register and Log In
In order to understand traffic to PCBGOGO.com and diagnose technical problems, we register or log in individual web requests. These registers and logs include time of request, individual IP address, referrer, and user agent strings. We may retain these logs for up to 60 days, although individually-identifying information will be obscured after one week.In order to prevent fraud and detect malicious behavior, we log IP addresses for specific actions, such as creating a customer account and placing an order. This information is automatically deleted after no more than 60 days.
Except case of malicious traffic, we will not share log data with any third party, unless compelled by legal process to do so.
Your Rights
You have the right to request to modify or to be removed from our active e-mail database list. To request changes, send an e-mail to service@pcbgogo.com asking to update certain personally identifiable information, or to be removed from our active e-mail list.
We will comply with your request as soon as reasonably possible. To protect your confidentiality, we can only send the request to the e-mail address listed in our database. We do maintain back-up copies of your personally identifiable information and reserve the right to contact former users of the website from time to time.
Shipping Methods
We offer the delivery methods which are DHL, Fedex, UPS and some China post.
Payment Methods
Payments for PCB and PCBA orders in PCBGOGO.com are processed through Paypal. When using PayPal on PCBGOGO.com, a user should provide their PayPal email address. The user will then be directed to Paypal.com to complete their payment, after which they will be redirected to PCBGOGO.com to complete the order. In case you do not have a Paypal account, credit card data including the cardholder name, credit card number, expiration date, CVV, and full billing address are sent to Paypal via an API for processing. Details regarding orders are not sent to Paypal. Each transaction is logged by retaining the transaction amount, the customer’s name, addresses and phone numbers. No any credit card data is stored at any point on PCBGOGO’s servers.
Privacy Policy Changes
Because of changing technology and marketing demands, we reserve the right to change our policies regarding privacy of your personally identifiable information in the future. If that happens, we will promptly update this Privacy Policy. We may also attempt to provide a message for our customers whose e-mail addresses we have, in conformity with this Privacy Policy, advising of any change(s).
We can be reached via e-mail at service@pcbgogo.com or you can reach us by telephone at +86075533066136, If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy, including if you feel PCBGOGO or someone within the PCBGOGO community has violated it, or if you are concerned about PCBGOGO’s general philosophy toward user privacy.
